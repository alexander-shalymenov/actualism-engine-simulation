Multiverse Variational Foundation
Principle of Actualization (A) ω = arg min (K(ω) - log p(ω))
In the Shalymenov framework, this variational principle selects a fact from the multiverse. Each independent cluster follows its own foundation G, potentially possessing unique physical constants.
Autonomous Branch Rate 10%
Controls how often a child separates from its parent cluster and becomes an autonomous branch. Each child still has an origin parent, but after separation its future growth develops independently. The maximum is 99% so each reality can still keep some visible continuity.
